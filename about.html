About Me

Hello, I'm Aurulil, a passionate artist dedicated to creating and sharing my artwork. My journey in the world of art began at a young age, and since then, I've been on a never-ending quest to explore and express my creativity. I have begun selling my work at conventions locally, and hope to grow large enough to vend at large conventions across the country!
